# Review Policy

## User Review Nodes

1. Choose which recommended opportunities to apply for.
2. Approve or reject the final submission package.

The second node is represented in the app by the final package decision actions: `approve_final_submission_package` sets the related opportunity to `approved_for_submission`; `request_revision` sends it back to `quality_blocked`.

## AI Decisions Between Review Nodes

The AI should make reasonable decisions about work selection, wording, CV emphasis, file naming, language, captions, and checklist order. These decisions are recorded in internal notes, not surfaced as repeated confirmation prompts.

## Chinese Review Requirement

All review files shown to the artist must be Chinese-first. English formal materials require a Chinese explanation of what the file says, why it was tailored, what risks remain, and what the artist is approving.
